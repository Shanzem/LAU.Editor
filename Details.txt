To test the program :-

Click 'New' from Editor tools

Choose 'Workspace 1' click 'ok'

Copy & paste io.write("Hello World")

Click 'Save' Check 'Workspace 1' and save as test.lua

Click 'Compile' type lua test.lua and press enter.

"Hello World" should be seen within the console.
